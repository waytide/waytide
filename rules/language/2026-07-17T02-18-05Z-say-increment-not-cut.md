# Say "increment", never "cut"

For a slice of scope or a release, say **increment** — never "cut". "this first increment", "this increment", not "this first cut".

**Why:** "cut" (as in a cut of scope or a release cut) is imported slang; "increment" states what is meant — a bounded slice of scope delivered as a step. Literal naming over slang (a case of the no-slang standard).

**How to apply:** call a slice of scope or a release an "increment", in names, prose, plans, and commit messages. Related: the no-slang rule and the name-literally-not-by-analogy rule.
