# Conformed the `loop-records` rule's filename format from the date-only `YYYY-MM-DD-<feature-slug>.md` to the full ISO 8601 UTC datetime prefix `YYYY-MM-DDTHH-MM-SSZ-<feature-slug>.md`. The rule predates foundation's file-name prefix rule, which names `loops/` among the directories it governs and says date-only-prefixed files "do not yet conform and should be renamed to the full UTC-datetime form in a conformance pass". DBE includes `foundation` and contributes `loops/`, so the two would have shipped in contradiction. The prefix rule governs the format; `loop-records` now defers to it rather than restating a competing one.
